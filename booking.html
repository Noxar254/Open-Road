Import Process Timeline

Importing a vehicle typically takes 4-8 weeks depending on the source country. Here's a general timeline:

  • Week 1: Vehicle selection and purchase
  • Week 2-3: Transportation to port and export preparation
  • Week 3-6: Shipping (varies by location)
  • Week 7: Port arrival and customs clearance
  • Week 8: Local transportation and delivery

Each import receives a unique tracking number that allows you to monitor the status of your vehicle throughout the entire process.
